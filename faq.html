FAQ

How old do I have to be to participate in this event?

You must be at least 18 years of age by April 1, 2016.

What should I bring to the hackathon?

Your laptop and charger. Additionally, you should be ready to learn, contribute, and make a difference for a good cause!

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24-36 hour programming event where people from various backgrounds come together, form teams, share ideas, and help each other build innovative projects. It is one of the best ways to meet new people, learn from mentors, and make cool stuff.

How are teams formed?

Teams will be formed in advance of the the hackathon by the group organizers based off of participants responses in their Eventbrite registration and the needs of the projects. Teams will be between 3 and 8 hackers, a coach, and an organizational liaison.

When will I find out which team I am on?

We will announce the teams on Friday during the Installfest, so make sure you are there to meet your team and start planning!

I'm a recruiter, can I attend your event?

If you would like to sponsor the Chi Ladies Hackathon, we welcome your sponsorship.
